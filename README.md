# Customer-behaviour

## Here are mysql queries on a data of a particular shopping area, on how a group of customers behaves across the year on the basis of shopping, subscription, discount applied or whatsoever.
## These queries complete almost every point of view on how an outsider see a shop or mall if he/she wants to analyse the activities across that particular area.
##These queries also helps the management, if they want the analysis of their whole year activity among their customers. So they produce more or less products according to these analysis so that they would always stay on highest rate of return.
